Privacy

This page is a template. Update it to match what your extension actually does.

What Sidecar stores Sidecar stores your extension preferences (toggles, layout choices, and UI settings) in your browser’s extension storage. This is used to keep your settings consistent across sessions.
What Sidecar reads Sidecar runs on ChatGPT pages to position and render its own UI. It may read parts of the page DOM that are necessary to mount correctly (e.g., finding the composer area). Avoid logging or persisting chat content unless you explicitly choose to support a feature that requires it.
Network access If your extension does not make network requests, state that clearly here. If it does (updates, telemetry, integrations), describe what is sent, why, and how users can opt out.
Optional modules Pets and Progress Quest are distributed as an optional “Fun Pack.” If the fun pack collects anything different, it must have its own privacy policy and permissions rationale.
